Pricing & Refund Policy

For mechanic shops and individual mechanics.

Marketplace Access — $99/month

Subscription includes access to local repair requests, estimate submission tools, and profile visibility in the ShopMyRepair marketplace.

  • Each request is capped at 3 mechanic shops + 2 individual mechanics.
  • You control pricing and whether to submit an estimate.
  • Winning jobs depends on estimate quality, response speed, and reputation.

Opportunity Guarantee (Refund)

If, during a billing month, your account receives zero eligible opportunities to submit an estimate, we refund that month’s $99 subscription fee.

Eligible opportunity means all of the following:

  • The request is within your selected service area.
  • The request matches services enabled in your provider profile.
  • The request includes the minimum details required to submit an estimate.

What is not refundable under this guarantee:

  • Opportunities existed, but no estimate was submitted by your account.
  • Missed quote windows, ignored alerts, or incomplete profile setup.
  • Not winning a job after submitting estimates.

Subscription Management

You can manage billing, payment method, and cancellations in the billing portal from your dashboard profile tab.
