Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for owners, mechanics, and shops.

Is ShopMyRepair free for car owners?

Yes. Owners can post requests and compare estimates for free.

How fast do I get estimates?

Many requests receive estimates within 24 hours, depending on provider availability and request quality.

Who can join as a provider?

Independent mechanics and mechanic shops can join and submit estimates.

Do providers pay to participate?

Provider pricing and policy details are available on the pricing page, including billing and access terms.

Does ShopMyRepair guarantee job outcomes?

No. ShopMyRepair is a marketplace for estimate matching and does not perform repair services directly.

How do I get help?

Use our contact page for support updates while we finalize email/live chat support channels.
